04 — ABOUT US

We make things that mean something.

OBSCURA® is a small creative studio built on the belief that visual identity is never superficial — it's the first and most persistent thing a brand communicates.

We work slowly and selectively. We ask difficult questions before we make anything, and we build visual systems that hold their conviction after the project end.
